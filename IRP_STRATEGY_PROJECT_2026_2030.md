## PART 7: RSU (KEYSIGHT RESTRICTED STOCK UNIT) INTEGRATION

### Should You Add RSU to Your Plan?

**SHORT ANSWER: YES, but with specific strategy.**

**Why RSUs are important:**
1. Additional capital source (not dependent on job)
2. Can accelerate retirement goal achievement
3. But: Concentrated risk in single company (Keysight)
4. Solution: Convert RSU to diversified portfolio

### Recommended RSU Strategy:

```
KEYSIGHT RSU HANDLING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

When RSU Vests:
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ STEP 1: Immediate Sale (on vesting date)               │
│ • Sell all vested RSU shares immediately               │
│ • Do NOT hold Keysight stock (concentration risk)      │
│ • Capture vesting price (eliminate volatility)         │
│ • Tax efficiency: Capital gains tax timing             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

STEP 2: Deposit to IRP/Brokerage Account
  • Transfer cash from RSU sale to Samsung Fund account
  • Or deposit to external brokerage if flexibility needed
  • Recommended: Deposit within 1-2 business days

STEP 3: Diversify According to Plan
  • Follow quarterly allocation targets
  • Deploy RSU proceeds in tranches (avoid market timing)
  • Example: If 100M RSU vests, deploy over 4-8 weeks
  • Split between growth and defensive based on current phase

STEP 4: Monitor Tax Implications
  • RSU is taxed at vesting (ordinary income tax)
  • Capital gains tax applies if stock appreciates after sale
  • Keep records for Korean tax reporting (필요자료)
```
